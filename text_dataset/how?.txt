1. We merged all CSV files from 2019 and 2022 whose filenames started with “lo”, “dep”, “an”, and “mh”, collecting every month’s data into four big category-wise datasets. 
(more_onlylonely.csv, more_onlymentalhealth.csv, more_onlydepression.csv, more_onlyanxiety.csv, more_onlysuicide.csv)
2. Each merged file now represents one mental-health category: lonely → depression-type data, dep → depression, an → anxiety, mh → general mental-health posts.
3. These merged datasets were labeled and combined into one master dataset (MASTER_DATASET.csv) to train our text-based models (anxiety, depression, suicide, etc.)
